# 🛒 Walmart Sales Data - SQL Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
SQL analysis of Walmart's weekly sales data (2010-2012) across 45 stores, exploring what external factors drive retail performance.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
1. Which Walmart store generates the most revenue?
2. Do holidays actually boost Walmart sales?
3. Which month generates the highest revenue?
4. Does temperature affect how much people shop?
5. Does fuel price impact shopping behaviour?
6. Does unemployment rate affect Walmart sales?
7. Which year was Walmart's strongest performer?

## 💡 Key Insights
- 🏆 **Store 20** is the top performer with $301M in total sales
- 🎄 **Holiday weeks** average 8% higher sales than normal weeks
- ☀️ **July** is surprisingly the highest revenue month — not December!
- ❄️ **Cold weather** drives the most shopping — hot weather the least
- 📉 **High unemployment** actually reduces Walmart sales — disproving the Walmart Effect theory
- 📅 **2010** had the strongest average weekly performance despite 2011 having higher total revenue

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **SQL** (SQLite)
- **SQLite Online IDE** (sqliteonline.com)
- **Dataset:** Walmart Sales Data from Kaggle
